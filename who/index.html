A Heritage of Technology and Business

Joel Barker

Principal and Writer

After a first career in telecommunications and IT, Joel Barker took on the communication challenges of marketing technology. For 10 years he has been writing and structuring content, largely for technology and B2B offerings. As Word Lions grows, he savors the experience of teaching other writers the Word Lions Way. If you criticize yippie small dogs, he will flash a patient, indulgent smile and later introduce you to Torito, the chihuahua that will change your mind.

Karl Steiner

Writer and Whip Cracker

Karl joined Word Lions in 2011 after almost fifteen years in sales and management roles for telecommunications, IT, and software development. Karl has a talent for accessible, value-driven content. He also speaks Saleperson-ese, which helps us connect marketing with sales needs. In his spare time, he likes to eat Greek salads and hang around in bars.

Erik Arneson
