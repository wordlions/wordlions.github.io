It has always been about turning on the lights.

The two principals of Word Lions, Philip Morgan and Joel Barker, separately moved from technology to content.

As network administrators and consultants, that light was the little green LED on computer that shows it is connected to the network.

Then, Philip and Joel began turning on the lights in people by writing technical procedures and delivering technical training. Today, Philip and Joel lead Word Lions projects where they turn on lights for entire markets. They teamed up in 2009 to create honest, influential, fierce content.

Word Lions explain complicated things.

  • We create content marketing that illuminates the value of your product.
  • We create training that builds competence in people.

Our experience with content, technology, and training turns on the lights for:

  • Prospective customers with content that can illuminate the value of your product.
  • Businesses and people with training that can transform.

Joel and Philip lead all Word Lions projects. With a combined 28 years of experience, they make sure that:

  • Customers get the right medium, tone, and level of detail in the final product.
  • Customers get the full benefit of our experience communicating difficult and challenging concepts.
  • Your project is managed by professional communicators who understand the unique challenges and risks of complex content projects. We know how to bypass snags and deliver on time.
  • The combination of our writing prowess and project management discipline facilitates on-time, on-budget projects with less stress for you and your staff.

The Extended Word Lions Pride

Word Lions work with a range of top-flight freelancers to help deliver excellent technical content to our customers. By using a network of established experts on an as-needed basis, we are able to keep our overhead low and value high.

Karl Steiner

For almost fifteen years The Other Karl served as a sales professional in the telecom and IT services industries where he focused on enterprise level sales. He later moved into a management role with a company providing application development services to the telecom industry. When the division he oversaw was disbanded and the functions re-located overseas, Word Lions presented the opportunity to realize a life long goal of writing professionally on a full time basis. Now, Karl brings to Word Lions the unique ability to create accessible value-driven content for highly technical products and services. He thrives explaining complicated things in a persuasive manner. In his spare time, he contributes to several entertainment blogs and enjoys playing chess with stray animals.

Karl Middlebrooks

Karl has been tweaking technology since the days of VisiCalc on the Apple ][+. He enjoys taking business puzzles and using technology to solve them, making people happy in the process. He’s also brought his love of writing together with technology, writing about everything from SQL Server business intelligence to integrating social network services and new developments in electroacoustic music.

Michael Kitson

Michael Kitson turns the knobs and pushes keys on a variety of intimidating devices to create high quality audio and multimedia production. In a sound booth, he is a soothing influence that brings out the best in voice talent.

Curtis Settino

Curtis has been creating visual and audio content for a variety of purposes since, like, forever. He’s helped create demos, courses, and advertising for some very large and some very small companies. His graphics help us explain complicated things with a visual language that is both casual and concise.
