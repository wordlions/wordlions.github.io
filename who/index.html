A Heritage of Technology and Business

Joel Barker

Principal and Writer

Joel started out as a telecommunications tech and IT administrator. He has been writing and structuring content for over ten years now, largely for technology and B2B offerings. Joel savors his new role teaching other writers the Word Lions Way. If you criticize yippie small dogs, he will flash a patient, indulgent smile and later introduce you to Torito, the chihuahua that will change your mind.

Karl Steiner

Writer and Whip Cracker

Karl joined Word Lions in 2011 after almost fifteen years in sales and management roles for telecommunications, IT, and software development. Karl has a talent for accessible, value-driven content.
